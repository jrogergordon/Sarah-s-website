My Fever Dream Recording

A while ago I was sick in bed. You and I had a phone call, late at night, and I couldn't sleep after. So I started making a voice recording that I figured I would send to you. First I made one that was a minute. Then I made a two minute recording. Then I recorded an HOUR! An hour of sick-drunk ramblings on everything from Spanish, talking to myself, and the depths of my love for you. While the things I said on this recording aren't more or less truthful than my sober expressions of love, joy, and spanish: something about how I felt brought my feelings out of me in decadent emotions. I really wanted to send you the whole recording but holy shit it's an hour long and half of it is coughs and wheezes. So I listened through a few times; found the best bits, and put them here for you to listen to!
